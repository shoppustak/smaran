Ship the §11 eval harness as a permanent repo asset: a CLI script that runs a labeled corpus
through the ASR + extraction adapters and computes per-field accuracy, extended per D-02 with a
fourth comparison dimension (extraction model alongside ASR provider) for the bake-off that decides
which of Claude Haiku 4.5 / Gemini Flash / Sarvam-M ships.

canonical vocab tables change, and it's the acceptance-bar gate (>=90% field accuracy) that decides
the eval outcome, not preference. The real 20-sample ground-contact corpus (native-speaker voice
notes, collected with consent during field interviews) does not exist in this repo — that's a
field-data-collection activity outside this plan's control, not a coding task; this plan ships the
harness tooling plus a clearly-labeled synthetic smoke-test fixture, not a real accuracy claim.
